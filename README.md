# 🌿 ARTIKA.life

> **One Platform. Complete Family Health Management.**

ARTIKA.life is a smart, family-focused health management platform designed to simplify everyday healthcare. It combines doctor discovery, AI guidance, growth tracking, vaccination management, document storage, reminders, and emergency support — all in one secure and user-friendly system.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 🧠 MediBot – AI Health Guidance

Get instant, reliable responses to everyday health-related questions in a simple and accessible format.

---

### 📍 DocMap – Doctor Discovery

Find nearby doctors based on location or pin code with quick access and navigation support.

---

### 👨‍👩‍👧 Family Management

Manage multiple family members under one account. Track health records, reminders, and documents individually.

---

### ⏰ Smart Reminders

Set and receive medicine and vaccination reminders to avoid missed doses.

---

### 💉 Vaccination Tracker

Track child vaccination schedules using age-based mapping.
✔ Shows Done / Due / Missed vaccines
✔ Sends timely reminders
✔ Download vaccination records as PDF

---

### 📊 Growth Tracker

Monitor child development using:
✔ Height & Weight tracking
✔ BMI calculation
✔ Height-for-age & Weight-for-age comparison
✔ Growth velocity analysis
✔ WHO-based reference ranges (non-diagnostic insights)

Provides informative growth status without medical claims.

---

### 🗂️ Health Locker

Securely store and manage medical documents including:
✔ Prescriptions
✔ Lab reports
✔ Insurance files

Files are safely stored with proper access control and metadata management.

---

### 🎮 MediPlay

Interactive educational mini-games designed to encourage healthy habits and awareness among children.

---

### 🚨 Emergency Mode

Quick-access emergency support with fast doctor discovery and contact assistance.

---

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

* **Frontend:** React, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
* **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js
* **Database:** MongoDB (for structured user data)
* **File Storage:** Cloud / Server Storage
* **PDF Generation:** Dynamic vaccination card creation
* **Scheduler:** Reminder system using cron jobs

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

ARTIKA.life aims to bring preventive, organized, and accessible healthcare management into one unified digital platform for families.

---

## ⚠️ Disclaimer

ARTIKA.life provides informational and tracking tools only. It does not replace professional medical consultation.
